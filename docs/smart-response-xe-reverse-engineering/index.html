Smart Response XE Reverse Engineering

I saw a Hackaday post about a class room gadget with a nice grayscale graphical LCD, full keyboard, and radio. People had found that it used an AVR microprocessor and had reverse engineered enough of it to make an Arduino library for it. I got a bunch of them on eBay and made a small contribution by creating high resolution scans of the PCB and uploading them to assist the reverse engineering efforts of others.
